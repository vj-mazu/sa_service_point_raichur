FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in Raichur do you serve?

We serve all areas within Raichur city including Station Road, Manvi Road, Gandhi Nagar, Sindhanur Road, and surrounding localities within 30 km radius.

How long does a home deep cleaning take?

A standard 2BHK deep cleaning takes approximately 3-4 hours. Larger homes or heavily soiled spaces may take 5-6 hours. We never rush — quality is our priority.

Are your cleaning chemicals safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use only hospital-grade, non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning agents that are completely safe for children, pets, and elderly family members.

How often should I get my water tank cleaned?

We recommend cleaning your water tank every 6 months. If you notice discoloured water, bad smell, or algae growth, get it cleaned immediately.

Do you provide packing materials for shifting?

Yes, we provide complete packing materials including bubble wrap, corrugated sheets, foam padding, and high-quality carton boxes — all included in the service cost.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

You can cancel or reschedule any booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time at no charge. If you're unsatisfied with the service, we offer a free re-service.
